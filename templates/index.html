Rules
  • Schools must register online here latest by 5th August.
  • Schools must reach the registration venue latest by 8:30 A.M.
  • Participants are advised not to take part in events with conflicting time slots.
  • Students are not allowed to bring their own laptops unless specified.
  • If any student is found guilty of using unfair means, the concerned school will be disqualified from that particular event.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • Winning schools must be present at the prize distribution ceremony else the prizes will go to the next school in the points tally.
  • Participants must be in school uniform.
  • Eligibility for events must be maintained.
  • Participants must take care of their personal belongings. The school shall not be held responsible for any loss or damage.
  • Participation in all events is mandatory to qualify for the overall position.
  • The registration form, latest updates, announcements and results will be posted on the Facebook page. [facebook.com/eSpice]
